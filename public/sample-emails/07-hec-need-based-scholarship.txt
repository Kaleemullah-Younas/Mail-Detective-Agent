Subject: HEC Need-Based Scholarship Programme 2026-27 - Applications Invited
Date: 2026-03-25

Dear Student,

The Higher Education Commission (HEC) of Pakistan is inviting applications for the Need-Based Scholarship Programme for the academic year 2026-27.

Programme Overview:
The HEC Need-Based Scholarship Programme aims to support financially deserving students enrolled in HEC-recognized universities to complete their undergraduate or MS/MPhil degree.

Eligibility Criteria:
- Pakistani national enrolled full-time in an HEC-recognized university
- Undergraduate (1st to 4th year) or MS/MPhil student
- Family income below PKR 45,000/month
- Minimum CGPA 2.5/4.0 for continuing students
- No other concurrent scholarship

Benefits:
- Full tuition fee waiver (paid directly to university)
- Monthly living stipend: PKR 12,000 (outstation students)
- Book and stationery allowance: PKR 5,000/semester

Required Documents:
- Attested copy of CNIC / B-Form
- Income certificate from relevant authority (Grade 17+ officer)
- Enrollment certificate from university
- Last semester's result card
- Parent's salary slip or income affidavit

Application Deadline: 20 June 2026

Submit applications through your university's financial aid office or at: https://scholarships.hec.gov.pk/
