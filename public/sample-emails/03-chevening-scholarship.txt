Dear Applicant,

Chevening is the UK government's international awards programme, funded by the Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office (FCDO) and partner organisations.

Who Can Apply:
Chevening is open to outstanding individuals with leadership potential. To be eligible you must:
- Be a citizen of a Chevening-eligible country (Pakistan is eligible)
- Return to your country for a minimum of two years after your scholarship
- Have an undergraduate degree that will enable entry into a postgraduate programme (equivalent to a UK Second Class Upper)
- Have at least 2 years of work experience
- Apply to three different eligible UK university courses

Scholarship Covers:
- University tuition fees (full)
- A monthly stipend (living allowance)
- Travel costs to and from the UK
- An arrival allowance
- Homeward departure allowance
- The cost of one visa application
- Travel grant to attend Chevening events in the UK

Application Deadline: 5 November 2026

Start your application: https://www.chevening.org/apply/

For Pakistan-specific queries: chevening.pakistan@fcdo.gov.uk
