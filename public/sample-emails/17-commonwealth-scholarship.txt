Subject: Commonwealth Shared Scholarship 2026 - Pakistan - Apply Now
Date: 2026-03-08

Dear Applicant,

The Commonwealth Scholarship Commission (CSC) in the UK is now accepting applications for Commonwealth Shared Scholarships for students from Pakistan for the academic year 2026-27.

About the Scholarship:
Commonwealth Shared Scholarships are funded jointly by the UK government and UK universities. They are tenable for one-year taught Master's degree courses at participating UK universities.

Who Can Apply:
- A citizen of a Commonwealth country (Pakistan qualifies)
- Permanently resident in Pakistan
- Hold a first degree of at least upper second class (2:1) standard
- Be unable to afford the cost of studying in the UK without this scholarship
- Be available to start your academic studies in the UK in September/October 2026

The Scholarship Includes:
- Approved university tuition fees in full
- Approved airfare: economy class return travel from Pakistan to the UK
- Stipend (living allowance) at the rate of £1,367 per month (London institutions) or £1,108 per month (other UK institutions)
- Thesis grant (where applicable) up to £265
- Study travel grant up to £1,200

Priority Development Areas:
Applications from candidates whose studies align with health, education, climate change, or economic development will be prioritised.

Application Deadline: 8 April 2026

Apply through the Commonwealth Scholarship Commission: https://cscuk.commonwealthofnations.org/apply/

HEC (Pakistan) acts as the nominating body. Also apply at: hec.gov.pk/commonwealthscholarships
