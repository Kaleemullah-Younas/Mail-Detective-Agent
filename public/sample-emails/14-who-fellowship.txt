Dear Applicant,

The World Health Organization Regional Office for the Eastern Mediterranean (EMRO) is accepting applications for its 2026 Internship Programme.

Programme Overview:
The WHO Internship Programme offers a wide range of opportunities for graduate students to broaden their educational experience. Interns gain insight into WHO technical and administrative areas.

Placement Duration: 6 months (August 2026 - January 2027)
Location: EMRO Regional Office, Cairo, Egypt

Areas of Placement:
- Health Systems and Infrastructure
- Disease Prevention and Control
- Environmental Health
- Health Information Management
- Communications and External Relations

Eligibility Requirements:
- Enrolled in, or have recently completed (within 1 year) a graduate or postgraduate degree in Medicine, Public Health, Epidemiology, Health Management, or related field
- Minimum age: 20 years
- Fluent in English (Arabic is an asset)
- Pakistani nationals are eligible

Financial Terms:
- This is an unpaid internship
- Interns are responsible for their own living expenses and travel
- Limited number of paid fellowships available based on merit - applicants may indicate interest

Application Deadline: 30 April 2026

Apply via: https://www.who.int/about/careers/internship
