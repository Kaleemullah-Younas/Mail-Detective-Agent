Dear Applicant,

The Asian Development Bank (ADB) is now accepting applications for the Young Professionals Program (YPP) 2026.

About YPP:
The YPP is a special two-year development program designed to bring talented, motivated individuals into ADB and develop them as future development leaders. Young Professionals (YPs) work with experienced development practitioners on ADB's most challenging and rewarding tasks.

Requirements:
- Citizen of an ADB member country (Pakistan is a member)
- Age 32 or under as of 31 December 2026
- PhD, or Master's degree with at least 3 years of relevant professional experience
- Degrees in: Economics, Finance, Engineering, Urban Development, Environment, Social Development, Private Sector Development
- Exceptional academic record

Compensation:
- Starting salary: USD 65,000 per year + international benefits package
- Housing allowance, education allowance for dependents
- ADB's comprehensive health and life insurance
- Retirement Plan

Location: ADB Headquarters, Manila, Philippines (with potential field assignments)

Application Deadline: 15 May 2026

Apply at: https://www.adb.org/site/careers/young-professionals-program

Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.
