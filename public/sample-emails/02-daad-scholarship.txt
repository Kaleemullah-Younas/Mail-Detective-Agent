Dear potential applicant,

The German Academic Exchange Service (DAAD) is inviting applications for its Master Scholarships for Development-Related Subjects for the academic year 2026-27.

About the Programme:
This scholarship supports talented graduates from developing countries to pursue a Master's or postgraduate course at a German university in a development-related subject.

Eligible Subjects:
- Economic Sciences / Business Administration
- Engineering and related sciences
- Agricultural and forest sciences
- Regional planning
- Social Sciences, Education, Law

Requirements:
- Bachelor's degree with above-average results (minimum 60%)
- At least 2 years of relevant professional experience after your first degree
- Pakistani nationality
- Aged under 36 at the time of application

Scholarship Benefits:
- Monthly stipend: EUR 934
- Travel allowance (lump sum)
- Health, accident, and personal liability insurance
- Study and research allowance
- Language course allowance if applicable

Application Deadline: 31 July 2026

Apply via: https://www.daad.de/en/study-and-research-in-germany/scholarships/

Contact: scholarship-pakistan@daad.de
