Dear Students,

Registrations are now open for the ACM International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC) 2026 Pakistan Regional Round.

About ICPC:
ICPC is the world's most prestigious competitive programming competition. Top teams from the Pakistan Regional advance to the ICPC Asia Regional and potentially the ICPC World Finals.

Contest Details:
- Date: 20 September 2026
- Venue: NUST College of Electrical and Mechanical Engineering, Islamabad
- Format: Team of 3, 5-hour contest, 10-13 algorithmic problems

Registration:
- Teams of 3 students from the same university
- All team members must be enrolled as full-time students
- Maximum 2 teams per university in the Preliminary Round
- Students who have already participated in the World Finals are ineligible

Benefits for Top Teams:
- 1st Place: PKR 150,000 + certificates + invitation to ICPC Asia Regional
- 2nd Place: PKR 100,000 + certificates
- 3rd Place: PKR 60,000 + certificates
- Top 5 teams: Internship fast-track at Systems Limited, 10Pearls, and Arbisoft

Registration Deadline: 15 August 2026

Register your team at: https://icpc.nust.edu.pk/register2026
