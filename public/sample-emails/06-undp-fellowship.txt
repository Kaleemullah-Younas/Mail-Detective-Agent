Dear Applicant,

The United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) Pakistan is accepting applications for the Policy Fellowship: Youth in Governance Programme 2026.

About the Fellowship:
This one-year fellowship places talented young Pakistanis in key government ministries and UN agencies to contribute to policy development and implementation. Fellows work directly with senior officials on national development priorities aligned with the SDGs.

Duration: 12 months (July 2026 - June 2027)
Location: Islamabad, Pakistan

Who Should Apply:
- Pakistani national aged 22-30
- Master's degree or final year of Master's (in Public Policy, Economics, Law, Development Studies, or related field)
- Demonstrated interest in public service and governance
- Excellent research and writing skills in English and Urdu

Fellowship Package:
- Monthly stipend: PKR 220,000
- Health insurance
- Learning and development budget: PKR 50,000/year
- Mentorship from UN and Government senior officials

Placements Available In:
- Ministry of Finance
- Ministry of Climate Change
- Ministry of National Health Services
- Planning Commission
- UNDP Pakistan Country Office

Application Deadline: 14 April 2026

Apply at: https://jobs.undp.org/pakistan-fellowship-2026
