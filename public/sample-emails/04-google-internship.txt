Google is now accepting applications for the STEP (Student Training in Engineering Program) Internship for Summer 2026.

About STEP:
STEP is a developmental internship program designed to introduce undergraduate students to careers at Google through a combination of coursework and project work. The program focuses on students from groups underrepresented in tech.

What You'll Do:
- Work on a real Google project with a dedicated manager and technical mentor
- Participate in formal learning opportunities (workshops, tech talks)
- Network with Googlers and fellow interns
- Contribute to products used by billions of people

Requirements:
- Currently enrolled in a Bachelor's degree program (2nd or 3rd year preferred)
- Minimum CGPA: 3.2/4.0
- Proficient in at least one of: C++, Java, Python, Go
- Strong problem-solving and data structures skills
- Available June - August 2026 (10-12 weeks)

Compensation:
- Competitive monthly stipend (USD 7,000 - 9,000/month depending on location)
- Housing allowance or Google-arranged housing
- Relocation assistance

Application Deadline: 30 April 2026
