Dear Applicant,

We are pleased to announce that applications for the Fulbright Foreign Student Program 2026-27 are now open for Pakistani students.

Program Overview:
The Fulbright Foreign Student Program enables graduate students, young professionals, and artists from abroad to study and conduct research in the United States.

Eligibility:
- Pakistani citizen currently residing in Pakistan
- Minimum 16 years of education (Bachelor's degree or equivalent)
- Minimum CGPA 3.0/4.0 or 70% marks
- Strong English proficiency

Benefits:
- Full tuition coverage at a US university
- Monthly stipend for living expenses
- Round-trip airfare
- Health insurance
- J-1 visa support

Fields of Study: All disciplines including STEM, Social Sciences, Arts, and Humanities.

Application Deadline: 15 May 2026

Apply online at: https://fulbright.usefp.org/apply
