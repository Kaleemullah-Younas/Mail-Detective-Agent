Dear Prospective Applicant,

MIT's Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) is inviting applications from exceptional international undergraduate students for the Summer 2026 Research Experience.

Program Overview:
The MIT EECS Summer Research Program pairs international undergraduates with MIT faculty and graduate student mentors to conduct cutting-edge research for 10 weeks.

Research Areas:
- Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
- Robotics and Autonomous Systems
- Quantum Computing
- Computer Vision
- Natural Language Processing
- Distributed Systems

Eligibility:
- International student currently in penultimate or final year of undergraduate degree
- GPA equivalent to 3.7/4.0 or above
- Strong foundation in mathematics, algorithms, and at least one of: Python, C++, Julia
- Letter of recommendation from a faculty member
- English proficiency: IELTS 7.0+ or TOEFL 100+

Program Benefits:
- USD 6,500 stipend for 10 weeks
- On-campus accommodation (MIT dorms)
- Round-trip economy airfare
- Access to MIT libraries, labs, and facilities
- Certificate of completion from MIT EECS

Deadline: 1 March 2026 (Extended to 31 March 2026)

Apply at: https://eecs.mit.edu/academics/undergraduate-programs/summer-research/

Contact: summerprog-eecs@mit.edu
