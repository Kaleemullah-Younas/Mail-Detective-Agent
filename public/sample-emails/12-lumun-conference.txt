Dear Delegate,

Lahore University Model United Nations (LUMUN) is proud to announce that applications for LUMUN 2026 - the 21st Annual Session are now open!

Date: 15-18 August 2026
Venue: LUMS, Lahore, Pakistan

About LUMUN:
LUMUN is South Asia's most prestigious Model United Nations conference, attracting over 1,500 delegates from universities across Pakistan and abroad. It is recognized by the United Nations Association and provides an unparalleled platform for debate, diplomacy, and leadership.

Committees Available:
- United Nations Security Council (UNSC)
- United Nations Human Rights Council (UNHRC)
- World Health Assembly (WHA)
- International Press Corps (IPC)
- North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO)
- Ad-hoc Crisis Committee
- Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC)
- 20+ specialized committees

Eligibility:
- Currently enrolled university student (any year, any discipline)
- Strong interest in international relations and global affairs
- Prior MUN experience preferred but not required for beginners' committees

Participation Fee:
- Single delegate: PKR 8,500 (includes accommodation and meals)
- Day delegate (no accommodation): PKR 5,000

Registration Deadline: 20 June 2026

Register at: https://www.lumun.org.pk/register

Delegation enquiries: delegations@lumun.org.pk
